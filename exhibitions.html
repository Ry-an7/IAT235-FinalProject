Current Exhibitions

The Vancouver Art Gallery is proud to be hosting MVRDV as one of five locations in their North American tour. See what makes MVRDV stand out from all the other architecture and design firms in the world, and how their designs stand the test of time. This current exhibition features two of MVRDV's signature designs from 2017, the Tianjin Binhai Library and the Baltyk Tower. Be sure to visit soon as MVRDV will only be here for a limited time.

Baltyk

Sept 10, 2022-Sept 20, 2022

Baltyk is a 16-storey multifunctional tower built in the Polish city of Poznan. This tower combines an office space, hotel, retail space, and restaurant into one building that serves as a figure in the city center. This multifuntional tower will change shape depending on the viewing angle.

Tianjin Binhai Library

Sept 10, 2022-Sept 20, 2022

The Tianjin Binhai Library is MVRDV's second project in Tiajin. This library is not only a educational center, but also a social space that connects a park to the cultural district is is located in. Peer into the "eye" of this library and see what makes it stand out.
